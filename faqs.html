Common Questions and Answers

What is Pelican Code?
Pelican Code is a student-run club focused on developing coding skills and securing internships at top tech companies.
Who can join Pelican Code?
Membership is open to all students at Tulane and Loyola universities with an interest in computer science, regardless of major.
How can I join Pelican Code?
You can join by filling out the registration form on our Membership page or contacting us directly at contact@pelicancode.com.
What are the membership benefits?
Members gain access to coding workshops, real-world projects, networking events, mentorship, and preparation for internships and job placements.
What types of events does Pelican Code host?
We host coding workshops, project meetings, guest speaker sessions, and more. Check our Events page for details.
How can I get involved in projects?
To participate in projects, you must be a Project Member. General Members can apply for promotion to Project Membership after one term of active participation.

How to Get Involved

Getting involved with Pelican Code is easy! Here are a few ways you can participate:

  • Attend our weekly LeetCode interview prep sessions and guest speaker events.
  • Join a project team to work on real-world coding projects.
  • Contribute to our blog by writing articles or tutorials.
  • Engage with our social media channels and share your experiences.

For more information on how to get involved, visit our Membership page.

Club Policies

Our club operates under a set of bylaws to ensure transparency and fairness. Here are some key policies:

Attendance

General Members must maintain at least a 50% attendance rate for the term. Project Members are expected to attend 100% of project meetings.

Voting Rights

General Members do not have voting rights on club amendments. Project Members retain voting rights on project decisions.

Promotion Eligibility

General Members are eligible for promotion to Project Membership after one term of active participation, or by majority vote from the board.

For more detailed policies, please refer to our Club Structure and Bylaws section.
