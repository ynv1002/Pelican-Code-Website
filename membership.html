How to Join

Interested in joining Pelican Code? We're excited to have you! Membership is open to all students at Tulane and Loyola universities with an interest in computer science, regardless of major.

To join, simply fill out the registration form below or contact us directly at contact@pelicancode.com.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to exclusive coding workshops and bootcamps
  • Opportunities to participate in real-world projects
  • Networking events with industry professionals
  • Mentorship from experienced coders and faculty advisors
  • Preparation for internships and job placements at top tech companies

Membership Structure

We offer two types of membership:

General Membership

  • Must maintain at least a 50% attendance rate for the term.
  • Does not have voting rights on club amendments.
  • Eligible for promotion to Project Membership after one term of active participation.

Project Membership

  • Expected to attend 100% of project meetings.
  • Holds voting rights on project decisions.
  • Qualified to work on Pelican Code sponsored projects.

For more details on membership requirements and policies, please refer to our Club Structure and Bylaws section.
